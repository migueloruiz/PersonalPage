About Me

A little infromation about me // Un poco de informacion acerca de mi

Miguel Ruiz / Mechatronic Engineer, Technology advisor and iOS Developer.


Born in 1993 in Mexico City, I hold a bachelor degree in mechatronic engineering from Autonomous National University of Mexico. I have experience designing wearables, interactive stands, virtual reality environments, video games, mobile applications and products. In addition I spend my time learning and developing personal projects. I targeted knowledge in engineering to design and manufacture installations, prototypes and products looking for synergy between technology and art. Download my Curriculum.
