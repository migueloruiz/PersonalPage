    Born in 1993 in Mexico City, I hold a bachelor degree in mechatronic engineering from Autonomous National University of Mexico. I have experience designing wearables, interactive stands, virtual reality environments, video games, mobile applications and products. In addition I spend my time learning and developing personal projects.
